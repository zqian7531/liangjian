两间室内设计工作室
创始人：黄思加&周迅超&徐丽艳
两间致力于打造个性化空间定制，承接各种店铺、餐饮、娱乐、零售、酒店等商业空间，以及办公空间、私人住宅项目。主张用自然朴实的材质与现代极简的建筑语言去融合，突破市场上固有的空间模式，结合创新型的思维方式去呈现不一样的空间效果。
Founder of Liangjian design studio: Huang Sijia & Zhou Xunchao & Xu Liyan
Liangjian is specialized in customized design of space, including commercial space such as shops, restaurants, entertainment, retail, hotel, and office, as well as private residence. It utilizes natural materials, merged with modern architecture language, to disrupt existing models on the market and think creatively to bring unique design.
